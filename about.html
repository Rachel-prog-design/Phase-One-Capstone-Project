About My Book App

Welcome to My Book App! This app lets users explore books and save their favorites in a personal list. It's designed to make finding, saving, and revisiting books easy and fun.

Features:

  • Browse a selection of curated books
  • Add books to your Favorites page with a single click
  • Remove books from Favorites whenever you like
  • Favorites are saved even if you refresh the page


Our Mission

Our mission is to make literature accessible to everyone by providing a simple, user-friendly platform where readers can discover, explore, and curate their personal book collections. We believe that reading enriches lives and we are committed to fostering a love of books in our community.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every person has access to a diverse range of books and the tools to build their own literary journey. We strive to create a vibrant community of readers who inspire each other to explore new worlds, ideas, and perspectives through the power of reading.
